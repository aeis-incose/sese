SESE 2019

The South European Systems Engineering Tour: May 2019!


1-Fees

The event fee covers attendance to all sessions, morning and afternoon coffees/teas, lunches, program and information pack.

Member of chapters have a reduced fee for attending. Non-members of chapters have a full fee price.

  • Members of the chapters (per event): 50 €.
  • Students (per event): 25 €.
  • Non-members of the chapters (per event): 100 €.

This year it is possible to only register for the certification exam. In this case you have to select the option "Certification" and register.

2-Certification*

Additionally, this year you can again apply for the INCOSE certification (CSEP) without paying any extra fee (see Certification).

If you cannot attend the event, you can even take only the exam for 25€.

The INCOSE representative for certification will be present in all the stops.

3-Registration

Registration conditions

Before being redirected to the SESE 2019 Event Brite web, you must ACCEPT the following condition:

  • I hereby give my consent to sponsors and partners of the SESE, in order to be informed about their products, services and offers.

If you are interested in the event or you have doubts please do not hesitate in contact us: sese@aeis-incose.org.
